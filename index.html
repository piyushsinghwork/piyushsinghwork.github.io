About Me

Who I am ?

Hi, This is Piyush. I'm a UI/UX designer based in Delhi, India. I use design to create beautiful user experiences. I have experience working in design firm and start-ups (as a graphic designer). In the beginning, as an graphic designer in a start-up called Paaswala Store, I learnt about designing field. I grew to be a designer who handles UX and UI: Visual design, solving problems, prototyping and iterating, and delivering the product at pixel perfection level.

Nowadays I am working with Design Dimension with the designation - UI/UX Intern so here my responsibilities are Visual Design, prototyping, wireframing and so on.

I understand the perfect user interface should look good and work even better. In short, I create beautiful online experiences.
