My Methodology

Due to my interest in design, I am quick at learning and applying new information. Below are the few main areas that my key strengths lie in.

Empathize

I put myself in the shoes of users to understand their needs.It allows you to gain an understanding of the problem you’re trying to solve, typically through user research. Empathy is crucial process because it allows you to set aside your own assumptions about the world and gain real insight into users and their needs.

Brain Storming

The concept here hinges on the generation of as many ideas as possible so that the we can later shift through these and reduce them to the ideas that seem most promising. And to explore all angles and realms; indeed, the best solutions can sometimes sprout from the craziest-sounding notions.

Concept Generation

We’ll work with you to take all great ideas and turn them into a focused, goal-oriented product strategy.The solid background of knowledge from the first two phases helps in this process to look for alternative ways to view the problem and identify innovative solutions to the problem statement we’ve created.

Design

This process means turning ideas into concrete examples.A number of scaled-down versions like sitemap, low fidelity prototype and high fidelity ,interactive prototype of the product is designed in this stage.

Analysis and Testing

This is the most important and interesting process and its reduces the risk of building the wrong thing.It’s not one time blessing, you need to repeat the process until the design is not confusing anymore and your users are able to achieve the scenarios you propose.

Deploy

Once the prototype validated. We can send the final design to get develop, launch and enhance the product.
